# OWK-002: Public Goods Funding Thread Series

Deliverable for bounty `owk-002`: five ready-to-post Twitter/X threads on the
history and future of public goods funding in crypto.

## Package

- `thread-01-origins.md` - why crypto adopted public goods funding
- `thread-02-quadratic-funding.md` - how quadratic funding changed grants
- `thread-03-retro-funding.md` - why retroactive funding matters
- `thread-04-protocol-guild.md` - funding maintainers and core contributors
- `thread-05-next-decade.md` - where the funding stack goes next

Each thread includes:

- 12 numbered posts
- a clear CTA in the final post
- a source block for fact checking and further reading
- concise language suitable for direct social posting

## Editorial notes

Tone: educational, builder-friendly, optimistic but not hype-driven.

Audience: crypto contributors, grant program operators, DAO contributors,
open-source maintainers, and funders who want a high-level but accurate map of
public goods funding.

Suggested cadence: publish one thread per day for five days, then repost the
best-performing thread as a long-form article or newsletter.
